Who am I

Mechanical Engineer Turned Programmer

My journey began in 2018 with a spark of curiosity during my first computing tutorial, which grew into a hobby over the holidays during my time at university. Over the following years, I worked on various programming projects as part of my Mechanical Engineering degree, completed a full-stack boot camp, volunteered my skills to help a few organizations, and gained valuable experience at a startup. Today, I am thrilled to be living my dream as a full-stack developer at Ntegra, a Software Consultancy.

Through these experiences, I have specialized in developing web applications using React, TypeScript, and Node.js, while also building competencies in Java (Spring Boot), Python (Flask, Dash Plotly), and C programming. Additionally, my volunteering experience developing WordPress sites has further broadened my web development expertise.

My drive to use technology for positive societal impact motivates me to continuously enhance my skills. I’m currently working towards the Amazon Cloud Practitioner exam to further my knowledge of cloud technologies, along with participating in voluntary projects to gain additional learning experiences. This dedication to improving my craft, combined with my willingness to learn and collaborate with others, positions me as a valuable asset to any team.

Aside from coding and volunteering, I enjoy tutoring, hiking, jogging, gaming, playing football, catching up with old friends, and watching anime and documentaries. I’m also a huge Arsenal fan who’s been relishing the team’s exciting resurgence over the past couple of years.

That’s a bit about me! To see more of what I can do, check out my portfolio or email me for my CV, which details my recent projects as a Junior Developer. I look forward to connecting with you!
