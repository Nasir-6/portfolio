Who am I

Mechanical Engineer Turned Programmer

Having spent four years studying bits of programming throughout my Mechanical Engineering degree, I realised that this is the path I want to take. Was my degree a waste of time? Of course not! If it hadn't been for my first computing module back in 2018, I don't know if I would have ever developed the passion and problem-solving ability for coding that I have right now.

So, what now?

I'm currently working on a project that I envisioned ever since the start of my journey - a Community Mosque Timetable App. I couldn't pass up this opportunity as it checked all the boxes for a challenge I couldn't resist - a simple concept with the potential to benefit thousands of daily users. Not only that, but it was a chance for me to build and deploy a production-scale application from the ground up using the latest technologies I was interested in such as React, Typescript, Node, Docker, and TailwindCSS. As someone who is passionate and curious about not only developing my skills but also using them to benefit the world around me, I could not pass up this amazing opportunity.

So what's next?

I'm currently in the latter stages of developing the application and releasing it, but I have also realised that I will soon need to get back into the industry to further develop myself. Hence, I am looking for full-stack roles within the tech industry. I hope to bring my curiosity, passion and experience to whatever role I end up in and continue to benefit the world around me.

When I'm not coding, you'll likely find me volunteering, tutoring, improving my Arabic, hiking, gaming, playing football, watching anime, documentaries or the ever-exciting Arsenal squad propel to new heights. You may even catch me going bald for charity! I'll be honest, my interests have been widening like never before due to my curiosity for the world around me.
